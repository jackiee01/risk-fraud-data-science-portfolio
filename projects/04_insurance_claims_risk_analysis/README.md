# Insurance Claims Risk Analysis

## Overview

This project will focus on analyzing insurance claims data to identify claim patterns, high-risk claims, and reporting-ready risk indicators.

## Business Problem

Insurance companies need reliable claims analysis to monitor losses, identify unusual claim patterns, and prepare accurate reporting datasets.

The goal is to analyze claims data and generate risk-focused insights that support claims monitoring and reporting.

## Domain

Insurance / Risk Analytics

## Problem Type

Risk Analysis

## Planned Workflow

1. Load and inspect claims data
2. Validate key claim fields
3. Analyze claim amount distribution
4. Identify high-value claims
5. Analyze claims by status, line of business, and settlement outcome
6. Create risk indicators
7. Prepare reporting-ready summaries
8. Translate findings into business recommendations

## Key Analysis Areas

- Claim frequency
- Claim severity
- High-value claims
- Settlement status
- Line of business analysis
- Loss monitoring
- Reporting-ready datasets
