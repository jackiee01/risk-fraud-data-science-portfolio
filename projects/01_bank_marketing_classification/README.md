# Bank Marketing Campaign Classification

## Overview

This project analyzes Portuguese bank marketing campaign data to predict whether a customer will subscribe to a term deposit.

## Business Problem

Banks often run marketing campaigns to promote financial products. However, contacting every customer can be inefficient and may increase customer fatigue.

The goal of this project is to identify customers who are more likely to subscribe and support more efficient marketing targeting.

## Domain

Banking / Finance

## Problem Type

Classification

## Target Variable

- `y`: whether the customer subscribed to a term deposit

## Planned Workflow

1. Load and inspect the dataset
2. Understand the target distribution
3. Perform exploratory data analysis
4. Analyze customer and campaign-related features
5. Preprocess numerical and categorical variables
6. Train classification models
7. Evaluate model performance
8. Interpret results from a business perspective

## Planned Models

- Logistic Regression
- Decision Tree
- Random Forest

## Evaluation Metrics

- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- F1-score
- ROC-AUC
- Confusion Matrix

## Business Interpretation

The final goal is not only to build a predictive model, but also to translate the results into practical marketing recommendations.

Possible recommendations may include:

- Prioritizing high-probability customer segments
- Reducing repeated contact for low-response groups
- Improving campaign efficiency through model-based targeting
- Identifying key factors related to customer subscription behavior
